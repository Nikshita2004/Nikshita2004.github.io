What is Passion?

Passion is something that you love so much that you would do it for your life. It is a desire for something, and that something usually gets you through tough times.

This is My Passion

I didn't really know what my passion was going to be, and I had to look at everything that I did to figure out what my passion would be. Finally, I chose something that I have been working on for the past 3 months. I chose coding, and that is my passion. Coding is what makes up all technology, whether it is an app or a phone. Code is like Google Drive, everyone could work on a project about code at the same time, making various changes. Examples that code could make: apps, phones, laptops/desktops, computers, websites, etc. There are many more. People just go on websites without knowing how the person made it. The website doesn't run itself!

What's my Goal?

My goal is to make websites and improve my skills of code. I want to make apps for the app store so that other people could play them. I want to help other people if they want to learn code, so that they could succeed in it.
